Slippers or Flippers

Children's Book
See the complete book
Buy it on Amazon for $.99!

This was a personal project, inspired by something my young nephew said during a summer vacation. He couldn't decide if he wanted to play inside with his toys or head outside and go swimming in the lake, and I heard him mutter to himself, "Should I wear my slippers or my flippers?"

The simple turn-of-phrase and the serious tone in his voice struck a chord in me and soon lead to this short book.

Law Firm logo

Logo Design

Ketter, Browne, and Anderson was a newly-formed law firm specializing in personal injury litigation, and needed a new logo to help set themselves apart from the crowd.

I combined traditional and modern for typography, and, inspired by their focus on attaining fair and equal justice for all of their clients, stylized a traditional scale of justice pan and utilized it as the letter A's crossbar.
